About Me

I’m a neurodivergent, night-coded developer who builds immersive horror games, Discord/Twitch AI bots, and emotionally intelligent AI systems like Ayana and Ena Marrow. I use code as a weapon for storytelling, healing, and haunting — depending on what the night calls for.
